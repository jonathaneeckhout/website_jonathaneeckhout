SOC/FPGA music player

A music player made on a Zybo board. It playsback audio files stored on a sd card. The purpose of this project was to use it along my other DJ equipment so it needed to be able to store cue points, speed up/down the song and play and stop a song. The Zybo has the zynq-7000 chip on it which is a combination of a CPU and FPGA.
